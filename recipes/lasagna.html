Lasagna

In under an hour you can have similarly delicious lasagna ready in your home. Follow the steps below and enjoy the ride:

Ingredients

  • 100g Flour
  • 200g Meat
  • 2 Eggs
  • 75g Carrots

Steps

  1. Peal the carrots
  2. Put together eggs, flour and meat
  3. Bake for 45 min at 200 degrees celsius
